Welcome Home

A Cathedral Rooted in Love, Open to All

Grace Cathedral has been a cornerstone of this community for over a century. We are a vibrant, multigenerational congregation that believes faith is lived — in worship, in service, and in the ordinary moments of everyday life.

Whether you are returning after years away, exploring Christianity for the first time, or simply looking for a place to belong, our doors are open and our table is set for you.

"For I was hungry and you gave me food, I was a stranger and you welcomed me." Matthew 25:35

Ministries That Move the World

01

Children & Youth

Nurturing young faith through story, song, and safe community. Programs for ages 3 through high school.

02

Music & Worship

Our choir and worship band welcome singers and musicians of all skill levels to offer their gifts in praise.

03

Community Outreach

Food pantry, housing assistance, and neighborhood partnerships — because faith without works is incomplete.

04

Grief & Healing

Confidential support groups and pastoral counseling for those walking through loss, illness, or transition.

05

Marriage & Family

Premarital mentoring, family retreats, and couples' enrichment to strengthen the bonds that matter most.

06

Global Missions

Partnerships in Kenya, Guatemala, and Southeast Asia — bringing clean water, education, and hope.
